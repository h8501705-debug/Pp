Academic Programs

Explore our diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to prepare you for the challenges of tomorrow.

Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence B.Tech +

A comprehensive program focusing on neural networks, machine learning, and high-performance computing. Students gain hands-on experience in our dedicated AI labs.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Focus: Deep Learning, Robotics, Cloud Architecture
Renewable Energy Engineering M.S. +

Specialized research into solar, wind, and hydrogen fuel cell technologies. Designed for engineers looking to lead the global transition to green energy.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Focus: Smart Grids, Sustainability Policy, Biofuels
Digital Business & Fintech MBA +

Bridging the gap between traditional finance and the digital revolution. Includes modules on Blockchain, Crypto-economics, and Algorithmic Trading.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Focus: Venture Capital, Digital Transformation, UX in Finance
Neuroscience & Cognitive Psychology B.Sc. +

Understanding the human mind through the lens of biological processes and psychological behavior. Features access to advanced EEG and fMRI facilities.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Focus: Brain Mapping, Behavioral Science, Mental Health Innovation
