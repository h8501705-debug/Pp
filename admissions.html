Admission process for the Academic Year 2026-27 is now live.

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate

Minimum 75% aggregate in High School (10+2) with core subjects relevant to the chosen major. Proficiency in English is mandatory.

Postgraduate

A Bachelor's degree with a minimum CGPA of 3.0/4.0. Valid scores in GRE/GMAT may be required for specific technical programs.

Application Process

01

Online Registration

Create your account on the Nexus Portal and verify your email.

02

Submit Application

Fill in academic details, upload your SOP, and choose your preferred courses.

03

Entrance Assessment

Appear for the Nexus Aptitude Test (NAT) or submit equivalent global scores.

04

Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a virtual interaction with the faculty board.

Documents Required

Ensure you have digital copies of the following before starting:

Academic Transcripts
Statement of Purpose (SOP)
Two Letters of Recommendation
Passport Size Photographs
Proof of Identity (Passport/ID)
Language Proficiency Certificate
